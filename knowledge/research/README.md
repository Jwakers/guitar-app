# Research

This directory stores reference material, notes, and summaries from external sources that inform the training methodology.

Documents here do not directly affect production code until their findings have been validated, reviewed, and incorporated into a whitepaper or skill/drill document.

---

## Intended Coverage

- Practice methodology (deliberate practice, spaced repetition, interleaved practice)
- Motor learning and skill acquisition research
- Sports science and progressive overload principles
- Periodisation theory as applied to musical instrument training
- Ergonomics and technique biomechanics
- Injury prevention for guitarists
- Music education research relevant to intermediate-level players

---

## Usage

Research documents should be summarised and cited when they inform a decision in:

- `knowledge/whitepapers/`
- `knowledge/skills/`
- `knowledge/training-blocks/`
- `docs/technical-spec.md`

Do not reference raw research documents directly in production code.
